Web Design - Fall 2014

This course will consist of in class work, class website, and a semester long personal website project.

Students are expected to approach the class with an eye towards their future professional roles while continuing to take advantage of the environment for experimentation that the classroom environment allows for.

Research and methodology will be emphasized.

Goals

  • Utilize grid, typography and modularity to construct their own design frameworks across entire web sites.
  • Create sites that responds to different devices and contexts.
  • Become proficient at manipulating HTML, CSS, and Javascript to realize their intent.
